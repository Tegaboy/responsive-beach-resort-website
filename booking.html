beach rules

Please note that bookings will be closed once the beach reaches 25% capacity.

Beach closes at 10:00pm on weekdays, while the beach closes 11:30pm weekends and public holidays. No entries will be allowed in after this time. Unredeemed entry tickets will not be refundable or transferrable.

Please be informed that external food and drinks are not allowed into the beach. Our various vendors offering different menu options will cater to your refreshment needs.

No loud music, set up or decor are allowed. A portable speaker is welcome, however please keep volumes to your group so as not to affect other beach goers. br No cameras/film equipment allowed, except payment has been made for photo/video shoot.

We hold the right to deny access or send you out if found with any prohibited substance or breaking any of our rules without refund of any monies paid.

If furniture booked are not occupied by 2pm it will be forfeited without a refund, and a re-use will depend on availability with 25% of the furniture cost as a change fee.
