About us

Summer Beach and Resort has been created with beach lovers in mind, to satisfy the need for sun, sand and surf - without the long drive, heavy petrol costs, and constantly being asked "Are we there yet?".

Big or small - Everyone can enjoy themselves by spending some quality time on our white, sandy "beaches". Whether you're here for a tan, you're looking to get wet, or you just want to enjoy yourself for a few hours, away from the hustle and bustle of city living, you can't go wrong with Summer Beach and Resort.

Please feel free to bring your own food and drink and enjoy a picnic at our resort. Don't forget to bring a gazebo or umbrella for a bit of shade. Summer Beach and Resort is experiencing the full force of the African sun this Summer!
